About Us

Hotel Ivory Inn – New Delhi, Located just 2 km from Delhi International Airport, Hotel Ivory Inn offers family rooms with AC, private bathrooms, free WiFi, and city views. Guests can enjoy multi-cuisine dining, from Indian and Chinese to Italian and local favorites, with options for in-room meals and special diets.

Facilities include a sun terrace, lounge, coffee shop, minimarket, 24-hour front desk, concierge, tour desk, and free parking. Major attractions like Qutub Minar (8 km) and India Gate (15 km) are nearby.
